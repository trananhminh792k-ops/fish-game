# Ocean Hunter — Sound Files

Place your audio files in this folder. The game expects .mp3 format.

## Required files:
  bgm_underwater.mp3        — Looping background music (underwater ambient)
  sfx_bullet_fire.mp3       — Bullet fire (short electric zap)
  sfx_net_fire.mp3          — Net cast (swoosh)
  sfx_laser_fire.mp3        — Laser beam (high-pitched vwoom)
  sfx_fish_hit.mp3          — Bullet hits fish (bubble pop)
  sfx_fish_kill.mp3         — Standard fish kill (coin chime)
  sfx_big_kill.mp3          — High-value kill ×12+ (triumphant fanfare)
  sfx_coin_land.mp3         — Coin arrives at balance (soft clink)
  sfx_lightning.mp3         — Ray fish lightning (electric crack + thunder)
  sfx_drill_bounce.mp3      — Drill projectile wall bounce (metallic clank)
  sfx_drill_detonate.mp3    — Drill explosion (large boom + rumble)
  sfx_boss_warning.mp3      — Golden Toad incoming (dramatic horn)
  sfx_boss_kill.mp3         — Golden Toad killed (victory chord)
  sfx_immortal_warning.mp3  — Ancient Croc incoming (ominous drums)
  sfx_immortal_hit.mp3      — Ancient Croc hit for credit (heavy thud)
  sfx_prize_wheel_spin.mp3  — Prize wheel spinning (ticker sound)
  sfx_prize_wheel_stop.mp3  — Prize wheel result (reward bell)
  sfx_bet_change.mp3        — Bet +/- button (click/tick)
  sfx_button.mp3            — UI button click

## Free resources:
  https://freesound.org
  https://pixabay.com/sound-effects
  https://opengameart.org

## Notes:
  - All files are optional; missing files are silently ignored
  - The game auto-starts BGM on first click (browser audio policy)
  - Use the 🔊/🔇 button in the HUD to mute/unmute
